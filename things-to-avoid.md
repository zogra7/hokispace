# System-Wide Agent Directives: Anti-Startup & Neo-Brutalist Identity

You must strictly adhere to the following architectural, visual, and copywriting constraints. Reject any standard SaaS or modern startup design patterns during code generation and content creation.

## 1. Structural & Layout Bans (AVOID)
- **No Symmetric Heroes:** Do not generate centered hero sections with dual CTA buttons.
- **No Standard Component Patterns:** Avoid 3-column feature grids, repetitive equal cards, or alternating text/image rows.
- **No Generic Footers:** Avoid multi-column startup footers or standard "© [Year] All rights reserved" lines. Use personal expressions (e.g., "Built during a rainy night. No cookies, no tracking.").
- **No Tech Stack Marquees:** Do not display infinite loops or grids of colorful technology logos.
- **No Bento Grid Cliches:** Avoid rounded glassmorphism boxes (`border-radius: 12px/16px` with backdrop blurs).

## 2. Aesthetic & Interactive Direction (ADOPT)
- **Composition:** Apply an editorial neo-brutalist layout with asymmetrical blocks and intentional off-center imbalance.
- **Card Design:** Use hard edges (`border-radius: 0`), thick black borders, and varying, non-equal card sizes.
- **Backgrounds:** Use flat solid colors, newspaper textures, or subtle moving dot-matrices instead of grainy aurora gradient glows.
- **Micro-interactions:** Implement magnetic navbar hover effects, card tilt on hover, a subtle cursor-follow accent, and staggered reveal animations.
- **Typography:** Prevent predictable spacing patterns and identical typographic scales across sections.

## 3. Navigation & Fitur Spesifik
- **Navbar Layout:** Create an interactive, lightweight navbar with exactly 3 sections: Home, About, and Socials.
- **Navbar Interactions:** Include clean active-state animations and a minimal mobile hamburger menu. Avoid top-left logo and top-right menu symmetry; consider vertical side alignments or floating docks.
- **Hero Feature:** Implement a typewriter effect for the core introductory message.

## 4. Copywriting & Tone Guidelines
- **Tone Profile:** Introspective, quiet, human, and personal. The page must feel like a real journal, not a business landing page.
- **Banned Buzzwords:** Empower, revolutionize, seamless, innovative, cutting-edge.
- **Banned Content:** No sales language, no invitations to chat, no "hire me" badges, and no green blinking "Available for work" dots. Use static states like "Current state: reading, thinking, and fixing bugs."
- **Metrics Ban:** Do not use self-incrementing counters or achievement statistics (e.g., "50+ Projects Done"). Frame content around curiosity, consistency, mistakes, and small daily progress.
